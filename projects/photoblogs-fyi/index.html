.fyi — Federate Your Instance

Find Real Photographers to Follow

The hardest part of any new network is an empty feed. photoblogs.fyi is where that gets fixed — an opt-in directory of real people running real photo blogs, found by humans and search engines both.

Discoverability is the Fediverse's Achilles heel, and everyone just lives with it. Not here. Opt in from your own blog and get listed — your photos never leave your server; we serve from your origin and store nothing. It's a photographers' community, so opting in takes a deliberate step: password and two-factor. Worth a moment's thought.

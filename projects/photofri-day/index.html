The Federated Weekly Photo Challenge

A weekly photo challenge for the whole Fediverse. One theme, every Friday; shoot it and #share it from wherever you already are.

The twist: your photo never leaves your site or instance. No uploads, no cropping, no compression, no silo. PHOTOFRI.DAY just points the world back to your work — the way it should be.

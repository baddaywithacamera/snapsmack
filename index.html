What Is SnapSmack?

It's a photo blogging platform you install on your own server. Every image you post is yours — sitting on your own hosting, in your own database, under your own domain. SnapSmack doesn't have an app to delete, a policy to change, or a feed to bury you in.

Post one image at a time with a title, description, and tags. Pick a skin. Done. It's deliberately not trying to be everything.

Built-in from day one

  • Self-hosted on any PHP/MySQL server
  • Multiple skins with full theme customization
  • EXIF data extraction and copyright embedding
  • Google Drive high-res download links
  • Static pages, albums, blogroll
  • Community features — reactions, comments, following
  • Bulk posting via companion desktop tool
  • Full backup and restore tools
  • 2FA login protection
  • AI writing assistant in the post editor

Companion Tool — Windows

Smack Your
Batch Up

Bulk-post an entire shoot in one go. Load a manifest, drag to reorder, assign categories and albums per image, embed EXIF copyright metadata, upload to Google Drive for high-res download links, and post the batch to your site — all without touching the browser.

Connects directly to your SnapSmack admin. Borrows your active colour scheme on login. Keeps the session alive through long jobs.
